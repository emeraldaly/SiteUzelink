Our Mission

Uzelink is dedicated to strengthening personal connections between Seniors and people that care about them. Uzelink ethically looks to provide meaningful insight into data that genuinely can enrich the lives of all involved.

Family Conversations

We live in a society that values independence. We want to honor that while creating natural space for important conversations.

Uzelink is geared to connect people so they can check in more regularly, forming habits that aren't centered on visits or problems. Through checking in more often and being aware of each others activities, adjustments can be made.

We want to make these topics more natural conversations.

Senior Issues

One of the biggest issues involved with aging is that although it is a natural part of life, individually we are disconnected from the implications of the process until we are too often faced with a dramatic circumstance. At that moment, time to research of often limited, and major life decisions are often made without necessary time to reflect.

Uzelink aspires to bring meaningful information to all parties involved in order to make educated decisions.

Physical Issues to address

  • Falls
  • Brittle Bones
  • Reduced Mobility
  • Cardiovascular issues
  • Immune System

Mental Issues to address

  • Depression
  • Dementia
  • Alzheimers
  • Denial of life changes

The Problem

The leading cause of senior injuries and death is falling down resulting from health changes due to aging.

Over the course of my military service, my father developed Alzheimers and my mother lost feeling in her legs and arms due to a mediction complication. In both instances, I had no knowledge of their change in health until I returned home - Ber Kirchner (co-founder Uzelink.

The Solution

Preventing falls by tracking activity and creating context for data driven conversations, resulting in happier and healthier seniors and creating peace of mind for their loved ones.

Product Solution

Uzelink delivers organized metrics, decisive points to you and the elderly people you care about. By notifying you when there is a meaningful change in their live, you no longer have to guess if your loved one is doing ok. Empowered with this information, you will have the data to act and create a healthy environment for them to continue to live in a way that keeps them free and healthy.

FAQ

Q: What if my loved one does not charge their device?

We have seen that even having the initial conversation with your loved one is a great first step. Sometimes it's as easy as reorganizing when they charge their device. Most devices will stay charged if they charge it every time they take a shower or when they are in the kitchen cooking dinner. Making the charging a daily habit may make it easier to use our app. Many devices can be set to remind your loved one, however, if they forget to plug in their device, this could indicate other issues that you may want to look into. Please take a look at our page on mental issues to better understand possible issues and ways to determine the next steps.
